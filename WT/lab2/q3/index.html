Definition list of html tags

The P.../P tag
Paragraph tag. Use this tag to make a new paragraph.
The HR tag
Horizontal Rule. Creates a horizontal line on the page.
The IMG SRC="image.gif" tag
Inline Image. Put the name of the graphic (.gif or .jpg) in the quotes.
The B . . . /B tag
Bold. Makes text bold
The H1 . . . /H1 tag
First-level heading large text size
